                    As an NGO named UDAAN, our initiative is focused on addressing
                    children's health care, financial issues, and education. Here's an 
                    overview of our initiative in each of these areas:
                    
                    Children's Health Care:
                    Providing access to quality healthcare services for underprivileged 
                    children.
                    Collaborating with healthcare professionals and hospitals to offer 
                    specialized treatments when necessary.
                    Raising awareness about preventive measures and healthy habits among 
                    children and their families.
                    
                    Mid Day Meal:
                    Providing meals during regular breaks in school.
                    Providing free meal to the needy people who can have their meal at our NGO.
                    
                    Education:
                    Promoting access to quality education for all children, regardless of their 
                    socio-economic background.
                    Establishing and supporting schools in underserved areas to provide a 
                    conducive learning environment.

                    Menstruation:
                    Providing sanitary pads in rural areas for women.
                    Creating awareness among people about the use of the sanitary pads and 
                    how it can be really helpful in avoiding harmful diseases.
                
                

                    As an NGO named UDAAN focusing on children's health care, nutrition,
                    mid day meals,education and menstruation,we will offer a range of 
                    facilities and services to support our mission and objectives. Here are some 
                    of the facilities that UDAAN provide:

                    Healthcare Facilities:
                    Medical Clinics: Establishing and operating medical clinics to provide 
                    primary healthcare services to children and their families.
                    Medical Support: Arranging for specialized medical consultations, 
                    treatments, and surgeries for children with complex health conditions.
                    
                    Mid Day meals:                    
                    Creating a centre for people to have free and good quality food.
                    Providing mid day meals in schools/colleges.

                    Educational Facilities:                    
                    Schools and Learning Centers: Establishing and supporting schools and 
                    learning centers to provide quality education to children from 
                    disadvantaged backgrounds.
                    Educational Resources: Distributing educational materials, books, and 
                    school supplies to students in need.
                    
                    Counseling and Support:
                    Psychological Support: Providing counseling services to children and 
                    families dealing with emotional or psychological challenges.
                    Parenting Workshops: Conducting workshops and sessions to educate 
                    parents about child development, health, and education.

Planning and Implementation Strategies

Collaborative Partnerships

We believe in the power of partnerships. By collaborating with other NGOs, government agencies, and private organizations, we maximize our impact and resources.

Community Engagement

Our approach involves active engagement with the communities we serve. We listen to their needs, involve them in decision-making, and build sustainable solutions together.

Data-Driven Approaches

To ensure effective planning and evaluation, we rely on data and evidence-based practices. Monitoring and evaluation help us assess our progress and make informed decisions.

Fundraising and Resource Mobilization

We depend on the generosity of individuals, corporations, and grants to fund our initiatives. Through transparent and ethical fundraising practices, we aim to secure the necessary resources.

Our Works

Health and Nutrition

At our heart, we believe that every child deserves to lead a healthy life. Through various engaging and interactive programs, we strive to educate children about the importance of balanced diets, physical activity, and overall well-being. Join us on this incredible journey as we empower the next generation to make informed and positive choices for a brighter, healthier future!

Education

We believe that education is not only to knowledge but also to the gateway of accomplishing their dreams. To achieve the milestone of 100% literacy rate, we need to inspire a love for learning, nurture curiosity, and foster critical thinking skills. Join on this incredible journey to shape tomorrow's leaders and change-makers, one child at a time

Mid-Day Meal

We believe that no child should ever have to study on an empty stomach. Our mid-day meal program is designed to provide nutritious and delicious meals to young students, ensuring they have the energy and focus to excel in their studies and explore their full potential. Together, let's create a world where no child goes hungry.

Menstruation

Menstruation is a natural part of growing up for young girls and should be embraced with confidence and understanding. By providing access to safe and hygienic menstrual products, we empower girls to stay in school and pursue their dreams without any barriers. Together, let's create a world where menstruation is seen as a symbol of strength and empowerment.
